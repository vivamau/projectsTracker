# Product Overview

ProjectsTracker is a full-stack project management application for tracking projects, budgets, vendors, purchase orders, and delivery paths within an organization.

## Core Capabilities

- **Project Management**: Track projects with budgets, health statuses, delivery milestones, and team assignments
- **Vendor Management**: Manage vendors, contracts, role rates, and vendor resources assigned to projects
- **Purchase Orders**: Track purchase orders, line items, and consumption against budgets
- **Organizational Structure**: Divisions, initiatives, and supporting divisions for cross-functional work
- **User & Role Management**: Role-based access control (admin, reader, guest) with JWT authentication
- **AI Assistant**: Natural-language queries against the database via Ollama LLM + MCP Toolbox
- **Activity Logging & Audit Trail**: Track changes and user activity across the system
- **Dashboard & Reporting**: Overview dashboard with charts, country-level views, and project health indicators

## Users

Internal staff managing a portfolio of projects — project managers, division leads, and administrators who need visibility into budgets, vendor spend, and delivery progress.
